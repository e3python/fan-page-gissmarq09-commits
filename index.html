About Mon Laferte

Raffael Jiménez - Flicker - CC BY-SA

Mon Laferte is a Chilean-Mexican singer and songwriter known for her powerful voice and emotional music. She blends genres like pop, rock, and traditional Latin styles, creating songs that are both modern and deeply expressive. Rising to fame in Mexico, she has won multiple Latin Grammy Awards. She is also a painter and outspoken advocate for social and cultural issues.

Top 3 Songs

Raffael Jiménez - Flicker - CC BY-SA

  1. "Tu Falta de Querer" - ~816 million+ streams (her most streamed song) This song is about heartbreak and unrequited love. It expresses the deep pain of loving someone who doesn’t return your feelings and the struggle of letting go.
  2. "Amárrame" - ~607 million+ streams, A passionate love song where she celebrates a deep, romantic connection. It conveys desire, intimacy, and the joy of being with someone who makes you feel alive.
  3. "Caderas Blancas" - ~544 million+ streams, A playful yet intense song about wanting to hold onto love tightly. It mixes desire and devotion with a sense of urgency, asking a partner to stay close and committed.
